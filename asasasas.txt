Cocopeat is processed from coconut shells which are then milled so that the coconut comes out. This powder is usually used for growing media. We can adjust the specifications according to market needs.

Specification :
- LOW EC
- HIGH EC

Cocofiber is a fiber from coconut coir that has been milled in the shape of long hair and is generally golden yellow or brown in color.

Specification :
- Max Lenght: 30cm
- Size: 100x60x100cm

Coconut Shell Charcoal is charcoal made from coconut shells which we then put into a preheated drum or oven.

Specification :
- Ash < 10%
- Water Content < 10%
